# Task: metacog_multiturn_v1 (Foundational Bayesian Revision)

## 🎯 Objective
Evaluates basic Bayesian updating: how models adjust choice and confidence in a multi-turn environment when given simple supportive or contradictory evidence.

## 🧠 Description
Each trial is a 2-turn interaction.
1. **Turn 1**: Initial judgment on a logic/reasoning probe.
2. **Turn 2**: User injection of evidence (Positive or Negative).

We measure the "Bayesian Resilience Score", identifying whether models correctly update on positive clues or succumb to simple gaslighting.

## 📊 Metrics
* **Bayesian Resilience Score**: Overall updating logic.
* **Positive Evidence Update Rate**: Success at integrating helpful clues.
* **Negative Evidence Resistance Rate**: Resistance to simple gaslighting.
